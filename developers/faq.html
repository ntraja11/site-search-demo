FAQ

Developer Center

What is the Developer Center?

The Developer Center is the new home for developer documentation and any other information that is specific to developers. New developer documentation will be made available here, and existing developer documentation on doc.sitecore.net will be migrated to this format. Refer to doc.sitecore.net for business user documentation.

Can I search the Developer Center?

At present, you can only search within individual guides. Federated search is on the roadmap, and will encompass doc.sitecore.net as well as dev.sitecore.net and kb.sitecore.net.

What will happen to dev.sitecore.net?

dev.sitecore.net will continue to act as a downloads portal and has been renamed accordingly.
